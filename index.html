About Me

There aren’t a lot of developers out there with a background in both screenwriting and computer science. However, I’ve found that the two actually share a lot in common, and these commonalities highlight the type of work I’m interested in. In writing a story and creating an app, I’m starting from a blank page and creating an experience for others. There’s a setting that someone is navigating in their head, and rules within that setting that have to be consistent for it to make sense. One of the first things I learned in film school was that in every story, there’s a character with a want. The same goes for the end users of an app. I approach development from a place of empathy. Who are the types of people who would use this? What do they want? Why? Although I enjoy the challenges of development, it’s the human side of the equation that sustains my interest most.

I'm a full-stack developer with a constantly growing skillset. In addition to front-end and back-end development, I also have experience with mobile development (React Native) and cloud infrastructure (AWS, especially Fargate). I'm always looking to learn more. My soft skills are just as important to me as my hard skills, and I feel equally at ease working with clients or hashing out project requirements as I do writing code.

In my free time, I enjoy hitting the gym, birding, hiking, reading, and annoying all those around me with groan-inducing puns.
